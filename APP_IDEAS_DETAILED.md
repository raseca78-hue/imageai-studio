# 25 High-Potential Micro-SaaS Ideas for 2024-2025

## Criteria Used:
- Problems with proven demand
- B2B focus (higher willingness to pay)
- Manageable scope for solo/small teams
- Clear monetization path
- Less than 3 major competitors

---

## E-COMMERCE & SELLER TOOLS

### 1. Cross-Platform Inventory Sync
**Problem**: Sellers on multiple platforms (Amazon, Shopify, eBay) struggle with inventory sync.
**Solution**: Real-time inventory sync across platforms with conflict resolution.
**Revenue Model**: $49-$149/month
**Why Now**: Multi-channel selling is growing rapidly
**MVP Scope**: 2-way sync between 2 platforms, expand later
**Competition**: Medium (existing tools are expensive/complex)

### 2. Profit Analytics Dashboard for Marketplace Sellers
**Problem**: Amazon/eBay sellers don't know true profit due to complex fees.
**Solution**: Automated profit tracking with fee calculations.
**Revenue Model**: $29-$99/month
**Why Now**: Seller margins are tight, need accurate data
**MVP Scope**: Amazon seller central integration + dashboard
**Competition**: Medium, but existing tools are expensive

### 3. Review Request Automation
**Problem**: Getting customers to leave reviews is manual and inconsistent.
**Solution**: Automated review requests via email/SMS with optimal timing.
**Revenue Model**: $19-$49/month
**Why Now**: Reviews drive sales, sellers know this
**MVP Scope**: Shopify app, email automation
**Competition**: Medium-high, but room for better UX

### 4. Returns Management Portal
**Problem**: Handling returns is time-consuming and hurts customer relationships.
**Solution**: Self-service returns portal with smart routing and analytics.
**Revenue Model**: $29-$79/month + per-return fee
**Why Now**: E-commerce returns are increasing
**MVP Scope**: Basic return flow + Shopify integration
**Competition**: Low-medium

## NEXT STEPS FOR EACH IDEA:

1. **Pick 1-2 ideas** that match your skills/interests
2. **Interview 10 potential customers** (not friends/family)
3. **Build landing page** with pricing visible
4. **Drive traffic** via relevant communities/ads
5. **Pre-sell** before building
6. **Build MVP** in 4 weeks max
7. **Launch** to waitlist
8. **Iterate** based on feedback
