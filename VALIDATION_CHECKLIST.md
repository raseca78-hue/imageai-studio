# Idea Validation Checklist

Use this checklist to validate any app idea before building.

## 1. Problem Validation

- [ ] Can you state the problem in ONE sentence?
- [ ] Have you experienced this problem personally?
- [ ] Can you find 10+ people complaining about this online?
- [ ] Is this a "hair on fire" problem (urgent) or "nice to have"?

**Where to look:**
- Reddit (search: "[industry] + problem/fed up/hate")
- Twitter search
- Amazon reviews of competing products
- Facebook groups in the niche
- Quora questions

## 2. Market Validation

- [ ] Are people already paying for solutions?
- [ ] How many competitors exist? (0 = bad, 100+ = crowded, 5-30 = sweet spot)
- [ ] What do competitors charge?
- [ ] What do users complain about with current solutions?

**Tools:**
- Google search for "[problem] tool/software/app"
- Product Hunt search
- G2/Capterra reviews
- App store reviews

## 3. Revenue Potential

| Check | Question | Pass Threshold |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| [ ] | Total addressable market | >$100M |
| [ ] | Willingness to pay | Competitors charge $20+/mo |
| [ ] | Customer acquisition cost | Can reach customers for <$50 |
| [ ] | Monthly searches | >1,000 exact matches |

## 5. Technical Feasibility

- [ ] Can MVP be built in 4 weeks?
- [ ] Can it be built by one person?
- [ ] Are there APIs/libraries available?
- [ ] Is infrastructure cost <20% of revenue at scale?

## 7. Pre-Validation Test

**Before building:**
- [ ] Create landing page with pricing
- [ ] Drive 100+ visitors
- [ ] Measure conversion to signup/waitlist
- [ ] Talk to 10 interested users

**Success Metrics:**
- 2%+ email signup rate = Good
- 5%+ email signup rate = Great
- 1%+ paid signups = Build immediately

## Quick Decision Framework

```
IF problem is urgent + market exists + you can reach customers + you can build MVP in 4 weeks
THEN proceed to landing page test
ELSE iterate on idea or find new problem
```

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ "No one is doing this" (usually means no market)
❌ "It's free to use but we'll monetize later"
❌ "We'll get viral growth"
❌ Building for yourself only
❌ Competing on price alone
❌ Markets with <1000 monthly searches

## Green Flags to Pursue

✅ Competitors exist but have poor reviews
✅ People are paying $50+/mo for alternatives
✅ You have industry connections
✅ Clear distribution channel exists
✅ Problem is recurring (daily/weekly)
✅ B2B market with budget
